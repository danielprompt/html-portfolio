The Best Movies According to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all time.

Avengers Endgame

This movie was intriguing and phenomenal. Just as the "Infinity War was," Endgame was a total masterpiece. Bringing together all the Avengers and placing them in one scene as well as other famous marvel characters. I was sad when Iron man sacrificed himself to save them all from Thanos. He was one reason why I watched Avengers

Rebel Ridge

This movie is so amazing. I watched it last month and I must say I had no regrets. One thing I respect the most about the main character is that he tried his best to resort to a peaceful approach the entire time. However, the antagonists wanted violence. I did not like how it ended though and I am hoping for a part2.

Spiderman Far From Home

This was a true work of art. Bringing all spidermen actors into one movie was just mind blowing. I have a feeling the idea came from "Spiderman Marles Morales." I hope a new spiderman movie comes out soon though.
